How to Access Your Courses

1

Choose a course

Click Open Course on any course below. The link takes you straight to enrollment — no browsing required.

2

Sign up with your district email

Create your account using your district email address — it takes under a minute. Returning? Just sign in instead.

3

You're enrolled — start learning

Enrollment happens automatically and the course opens right away. Your progress saves as you go — return anytime by signing back in.
